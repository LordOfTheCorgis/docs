Lumi-Panel is Lumix Solutions' custom build of the Pterodactyl game panel. It's the web interface you use to control your game servers, application deployments, and voice infrastructure hosted with us.

The sidebar is split into three sections:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="General" icon="circle-info">
    Day-to-day controls: the live **Console**, server **Settings**, and the **Activity** audit log.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Management" icon="folder-gear">
    Server data and connectivity: **Files**, **Databases**,  and the **Server Importer**.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Configuration" icon="sliders">
    How your server runs and who can touch it: **Schedules**, **Users**, and **Startup**.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Accessing Lumi-Panel

Lumi-Panel is available at your panel URL provided after checkout. Sign in with the credentials tied to your Lumix Solutions account.

<Note>
  If you can't sign in, check the [status page](https://lumixsolutions.org) or reach out in our Discord - Support is staffed 24/7 by engineers, not script readers.
</Note>

## What's next

Each tab in the sidebar has its own page in this section. Start with the [Console](/lumi-panel/console) if you just want to boot a server, or jump to [Files](/lumi-panel/files) to upload your configs.
