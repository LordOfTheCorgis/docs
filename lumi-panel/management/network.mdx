The **Network** tab shows the IP addresses and ports allocated to your server.

## Primary allocation

Every server has one **primary allocation** — the IP and port your game/app listens on by default. This is what you give players to connect.

You can:

- **Set an alias** — a friendly label (e.g. `main`, `voice`, `query`).
- **Make primary** — promote a different allocation to primary (the server must be restarted for this to take effect).

## Need more allocations?

Your server already ships with the allocations its game needs out of the box. If you need an extra port — for a BungeeCord listener, a query port, a second FiveM instance, etc. — [open a support ticket](https://lumixsolutions.org) or ping us in Discord. A real engineer will provision it, usually within minutes.

## DDoS protection

Every allocation on every Lumix Solutions node is routed through our 10 Tbps\+ scrubbing layer with intelligent traffic analysis. There's nothing to enable — it's on by default and free.

<Note>
  Need a dedicated IP, custom port range, or anycast routing? Same flow — open a ticket. We provision these when the use case calls for it, not as an upsell.
</Note>
