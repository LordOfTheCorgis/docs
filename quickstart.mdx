This guide takes you from checkout to a running server in just a few minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:

- A [Lumix Solutions account](https://billing.lumixsolutions.org)
- An active hosting plan for the game or app you want to run
- Your panel credentials, sent to you after checkout

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Order your server">
    Pick a plan from the [Billing Portal](https://billing.lumixsolutions.org) and complete checkout. Your server is provisioned automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in to Lumi-Panel">
    Head to the [Server Panel](https://panel.lumixsolutions.org) and sign in with the credentials tied to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure and start">
    Open your server, set your **Startup** options, upload any files you need from the **Files** tab, then hit **Start** on the **Console**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
